## Tax Benefits (Make Returns Tax-Efficient)

### Home Loan Interest Deduction (Section 24)

- Up to ₹2L per annum deductible on interest
- Example: ₹60L property, 8% loan = ₹4.8L interest, ₹2L deductible
- Tax saved (30% bracket): ₹60K/year
- **Effective loan cost: 6.5% (vs 8% nominal)**

### Depreciation Benefit (Building Depreciation)

- 5% per annum on property cost
- Example: ₹60L property = ₹3L depreciation in Year 1
- Depreciable for 40 years
- Reduces taxable rental income significantly

### Capital Gains Exemption (Section 54)

- Hold for 2+ years = LTCG (20% tax)
- Reinvest in new residential property = **0 tax** on gains
- Example: Buy ₹60L, sell ₹95L after 7 years (₹35L gain)
- Reinvest ₹95L = Entire ₹35L gain exempt from tax

### Standard Deduction

- 30% of rental income automatically deductible
- Example: ₹6L rental income = ₹1.8L auto-deduction
- No receipts needed
