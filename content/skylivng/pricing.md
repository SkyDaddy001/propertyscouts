## Tax Benefits: Government Supports Your Purchase

### Home Loan Interest Deduction (Section 24)

**Annual Deduction:** ₹2L maximum on home loan interest

**Calculation Example (₹62L property, 8% loan):**
- Total interest in Year 1: ₹39.8K
- Government allows you to deduct: ₹39.8K (all of it)
- Tax saved: ₹39.8K × 30% = ₹12K/year
- **Effective loan cost drops to 7.7% (vs 8% nominal)**

**Over 20 Years:**
- Total interest paid: ₹39.2L
- Tax savings (₹2L cap/year applies): ₹12L-15L lifetime
- **Net interest cost to you: ₹24-27L (not ₹39L)**
