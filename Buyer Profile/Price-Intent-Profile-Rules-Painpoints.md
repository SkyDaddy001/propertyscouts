# Price-Intent Buyer Profile: Rules, Benefits & Pain Points

---

## 1. WHO IS THE PRICE-INTENT BUYER TYPE?

**Profile:**
- Age: 25-40 years (first-time buyers, middle-income earners)
- Mindset: Value for money, affordability, long-term investment potential
- Investment Ticket: ₹45L - ₹75L
- Intent: Own home within budget, building net worth, avoiding rent
- Risk Profile: Conservative (price sensitive, but want safety)
- Decision Factor: Affordability first, then ROI, then location

**Income Profile:**
- Middle-class professionals (₹40L-80L annual income)
- Salary-based employment (not business)
- Dual-income households (both parents working)
- Limited savings for down payment (8-15% instead of 20%)

**Geographic Origin:** Tier-2 cities + metro outskirts (Chennai, Coimbatore, Pune, Nagpur, Lucknow)

## 2. HOME LOAN TAX BENEFITS FOR BUDGET BUYERS

### Interest Deduction (₹2L Cap)

**Applicable to:** First-time home loan

**Tax Benefit:**
- Reduces taxable income by ₹2L annually
- 30% tax slab saves ₹60K/year (₹5K/month)
- Important for budget buyers (every rupee counts)

**Example:**
- Property: ₹65L
- Down payment: ₹8L (12%)
- Loan: ₹57L at 8% for 20 years
- EMI: ₹46.6K/month
- Year 1 interest: ₹4.56L
- Deductible: ₹2L (capped)
- Tax saved: ₹60K/year = ₹5K/month
- **Effective EMI: ₹41.6K/month** (after tax benefit)

---

### Principal Reduction (Builds Equity)

**Key Benefit for Budget Buyers:** Every EMI builds ownership

**Equity Progress:**
- Year 1: Equity = ₹5.6L (out of ₹57L loan)
- Year 5: Equity = ₹29L
- Year 10: Equity = ₹57L (half paid, home fully yours)

**Peace of Mind:** "After 10 years, I own home outright, zero mortgage"

---

### PMAY Benefits (Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana)

**Government Subsidy for First-Time Buyers:**

**Eligibility:**
- First-time home buyer
- Annual income < ₹18L (for ₹40L home subsidy)
- Property < ₹45L
- All major metros included (PMAY-U scheme)

**Subsidy Amount:**
- Up to ₹2.67L interest subsidy (during first 5 years of loan)
- Effectively reduces effective loan interest rate by 3-4%

**Example:**
- Loan: ₹52L at 8%
- With PMAY subsidy: Effective rate = 4-5%
- Monthly interest reduction: ₹800-1000
- **Total 5-year subsidy: ₹2.67L**

**Process:**
- Loan through PMAY-approved bank
- Bank claims subsidy from government
- Borrower gets immediate benefit (lower EMI)
