## 2. INVESTMENT STRUCTURE & TAX BENEFITS

### Personal Property Investment (Individual)

**How Most Investors Buy:**
- Buy in individual name (self or spouse)
- Take home loan in individual name
- Rent out property, earn rental income
- Sell after 5-7 years for capital appreciation

**Tax Benefits:**
1. **Home Loan Interest Deduction (Section 24):**
   - Up to ₹2L per annum deductible from rental income
   - Reduces taxable rental income by 40-60%
   - Example: ₹60L property, ₹48L loan at 8%, ₹3.84L annual interest
   - Deductible: ₹2L (capped), non-deductible: ₹1.84L

2. **Depreciation Deduction (Section 23):**
   - Building depreciation: 5% per annum on cost
   - Example: ₹60L property = ₹3L depreciation in Year 1
   - Reduces taxable rental income significantly

3. **Standard Deduction:**
   - 30% of rental income automatically deductible
   - Example: ₹6L rental income, ₹1.8L auto-deduction

4. **Capital Gains Exemption (Section 54):**
   - Hold property for 2+ years = Long-term capital gains (20%)
   - Reinvest sale proceeds in new residential property = Exempt from capital gains tax
   - Example: Sell for ₹1Cr (bought ₹60L), gain ₹40L, reinvest ₹1Cr in new property = 0 tax on ₹40L gain

5. **Section 54F (Capital Gains Account):**
   - Instead of reinvesting in property, deposit sale proceeds in capital gains account
   - Deploy within 12 months in residential property
   - Flexible timing for reinvestment

---

### Corporate/HUF Investment Structure

**Option 1: Hindu Undivided Family (HUF)**
- Better tax efficiency for families
- Separate ITR filing
- Can split ownership among family members
- Marginal tax rate lower than individual rate

**Option 2: Partnership Firm or LLP**
- Multiple investors pool capital
- Shared ownership
- Complex but can optimize taxes across partners
- Requires compliance filings

**Most Common:** Individual ownership (70% of investors) + HUF structure (20%) + Corporate (10%)

- Many developers target end-users, not investors
- Properties in metros (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore) have yields only 3-4%
- Difficult to calculate true net yield due to unknown maintenance costs

**How Home Realtors Solves This:**
- ✅ Pre-vetted properties: Only list properties with guaranteed 8-12% rental yield
- ✅ "Investor Yield Calculator" on website: Input property price, loan amount, get exact yield
- ✅ Rental demand guarantee: "We can guarantee pre-lease arrangements for institutional buyers"
- ✅ Maintenance transparency: Show exact maintenance costs (negotiated with builders)

## 9. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

### RERA Compliance for Investors
- Projects must be RERA registered
- Escrow accounts protect investor capital
- Guaranteed project completion timelines
- Investor grievance redressal available

### ITR Filing Requirements
- Must file ITR-2 (or ITR-3 if business income)
- Attach Form 16A (TDS on rental income if applicable)
- Claim depreciation, loan interest, maintenance deductions
- File before July 31 to claim losses against other income

### Capital Gains Reporting
- File ITR with capital gains details
- Attach property valuation report
- Show cost of acquisition + sale proceeds
- Claim exemption under Section 54 if applicable
